Why Coding Club ?

It is an initiative by the students of CSE and ICT branch which were the first batch of our institute to be passed out in 2020. Slowly and steadily, this club created a chaos in the minds of the students regarding coding and other related things, which laterly became a vast community consisting of professors and other outsiders alongwith students.

What do we do???

We conduct events and workshops, hold lectures and talks, and even host coding competitions and hackathons. There is something for everyone. Whether you want to learn, participate in hackathons and win big prizes, prepare for the Google Summer of Code or code your way through the ACM-ICPC, you can do it all here.
